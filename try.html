My Life Story

I am Nathaniel Cunanan, currently a 2nd-year student in University of the Philippines Los Baños. Right now, I am trying to learn a lot of concepts with regards to Computer Science, with guidance from our respective ICS instructors. For now, I would say that I am enjoying my stay here, most especially that I am finally learning about the insides of what I usually encounter on a day-to-day basis, as I play games, interact with other people through online means, and also study for my future.

Ever since I was young, I was already exposed to computer. And such is most probably the reason why I decided to pursue this degree program. In line with this, I am really excited to get to learn more about the deeper concepts that I will need in order to further understand this digitalized world. With the constant advancement in technology, it really is a must for people to get to learn about the basics, and me, being able to really understand it at a deeper level, will surely help in the near future, at least in one way or another.
Having been aware of how this journey is not so easy at all, I've come to realize a lot of things. One of which is the essence of coding and programming. Features that we can see regularly are not done in just the blink of an eye. It takes a lot of time and effort from passionate people to actually get something to work, and truly, I am thankful for them. Sooner or later, I am hoping to also be the one to provide such services to people - being able to use what I learn as an undergraduate in BS Computer Science to make the lives of everyone a little bit better.
